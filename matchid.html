We envision a future where every individual has full autonomy and sovereignty over their data, identity, and digital presence.

Create MatchID

What is MatchID?

MatchID redefines digital identity with a low-threshold decentralized identity (DID) registration system that effortlessly imports Web2 users and bridges interactions across multiple blockchains.

Compatible with both EVM and MOVE, MatchID ensures full interoperability and can be seamlessly integrated into  any protocol within 10 minutes using our white-labeled SDK

The CURRENT PROBLEM

In the Web2 ecosystem, giants like Google and Meta harvest and monetize your data without clear consent or transparency. This often leaves users in the dark about how their information is used.

MatchID transforms this dynamic by giving you full control over your data and identity. Every time your data is shared, it requires your consent. Moreover, we share the revenue generated from your data back to you, recognizing your contribution to the digital economy. This model ensures that your data remains private, secure, and profitable for you.

Technology and Security

At the core of MatchID is our commitment to security. We utilize state-of-the-art zero-knowledge proofs and homomorphic encryption to ensure that your identity and data remain private and secure. This advanced technology underpins a system where your personal information is safeguarded against unauthorized access and breaches.

Shifting the Balance of Power

By adopting MatchID, the power dynamic shifts dramatically from centralized platforms to the individual user. This shift enhances your autonomy over online content and interactions, mitigating risks associated with censorship and data misuse. For businesses, it offers access to a rich, consent-based analytics framework that improves decision-making and user engagement.
